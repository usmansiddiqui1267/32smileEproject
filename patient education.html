Everything you need to know before your next cleaning visit

1. What is Teeth Cleaning?
  • Removes plaque and tartar.
  • Cleans around the gum line and between teeth.
2. Why is it Important?
  • Prevents gum disease.
  • Eliminates bad breath.
  • Improves oral hygiene.
  • Makes teeth brighter.
3. How is the Procedure Done?
  • Ultrasonic scaler removes deposits.
  • Manual tools clean deeper areas.
  • Polishing smooths the teeth surface.
  • Fluoride treatment for extra protection.
4. Is It Painful?
  • Generally not painful.
  • Mild discomfort if gums are sensitive.
  • Numbing gel can be used if needed.
5. Frequency
  • Every 6 months.

6. Post-Cleaning Care
  • Avoid hot/cold foods for a few hours.
  • Brush & floss daily.
  • Attend regular checkups.
